Our Story

Born in 2025, Built for the World

Founded in 2025 in Abuja by Muhammad Hadi — popularly known as Dollahead — MHU Travel & Tour was built from the ground up with a single obsession: making premium travel experiences accessible to every Nigerian. What started with a bold vision and a WhatsApp number has quickly grown into one of Abuja's most talked-about travel brands.

We understand the local traveller's unique challenges: visa guidance, documentation, budget planning, and the assurance that someone experienced is managing every detail. Whether it's a honeymoon in the Maldives or a sacred Umrah pilgrimage, MHU handles it all.

Meet Our Founder & CEO

Muhammad Hadi

Founder & CEO  ·  "Dollahead"

Known widely as Dollahead, Muhammad Hadi founded MHU Travel & Tour in 2025 with one clear mission — to make world-class travel accessible to every Nigerian. A certified travel consultant and IATA-trained professional, he brings a rare blend of hustle and industry expertise to every client relationship. From negotiating premium hotel rates to personally walking clients through visa applications, Muhammad is deeply hands-on. Under his leadership, MHU has grown from a one-man operation into one of Abuja's most trusted travel brands — and the cornerstone of the MHU Global Investment group.
