Our Story

Born in 2025, Built for the World

Founded in 2025 in Abuja by Muhammad Hadi — popularly known as Dollahead — MHU Travel & Tour was built from the ground up with a single obsession: making premium travel experiences accessible to every Nigerian. What started with a bold vision and a WhatsApp number has quickly grown into one of Abuja's most talked-about travel brands.

We understand the local traveller's unique challenges: visa guidance, documentation, budget planning, and the assurance that someone experienced is managing every detail. Whether it's a honeymoon in the Maldives or a sacred Umrah pilgrimage, MHU handles it all.
