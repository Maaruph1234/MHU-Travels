Our Story

Nine Years of Taking You Places

Founded in 2015 in Abuja, MHU Travel & Tour started as a small team with a big dream — to help Nigerians experience the world without the hassle. What began with a handful of Dubai packages has grown into a full-service travel agency serving over 1,200 clients across 40+ destinations worldwide.

We understand the local traveller's unique needs: visa guidance, documentation support, budget-friendly options, and the assurance that someone experienced is managing every detail of your trip.
